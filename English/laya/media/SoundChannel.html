Classpublic class SoundChannel
InheritanceSoundChannel EventDispatcher Object

SoundChannel Used to control the sound in a program. Each voice is allocated to a vocal tract, and the application can have multiple vocal tract mixed together.

SoundChannel The class contains methods for controlling the playing, pause, stop, and volume of the sound, as well as the means of obtaining the playback state of the sound, the total time, the current play time, the total number of times, the playback address, and so on.
